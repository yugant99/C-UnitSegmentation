ROOT CAUSE ANALYSIS
================================================================================

PHONETIC MISMATCHES (6 instances, 11% of total):
-------------------------------------------------
Pattern: "There's, um..." in Descript → "There's (mm [FP])" in Gold

Why it happens:
  • "um" and "mm" are phonetically similar hesitation sounds
  • Human transcriber heard/wrote "mm"
  • Descript ASR transcribed as "um"  
  • Both are valid representations of same vocal sound
  • Impossible to predict which one human will choose

Affected: VR_Call_Me_by_Your_Name (2), VR_Talk_To_Me (2), VR_GhostRider (1), 
          VR_Django_Unchained (1)

Solution: UNFIXABLE - cannot predict human transcriber's phonetic choice


COMPLETELY MISSING DATA (8 instances, 15% of total):
-----------------------------------------------------
Pattern: Entire Avatar responses absent from Descript

Why it happens:
  • Descript ASR may skip low-confidence utterances
  • Background noise or unclear speech
  • Overlapping speech not captured
  • ASR filtering out incomplete/hesitant speech

Affected: VR_Howl's_Moving_Castle (7), VR_Spirited_Away (1)

Solution: UNFIXABLE - cannot detect what isn't in input data


FP WORDS MISSING (40 instances, 74% of total):
------------------------------------------------
Pattern: Gold has complex disfluent speech, Descript has simple words

Examples:
  Gold:     "(Um [FP]) (I) (um [FP]) (um [FP]) (I) I need to go."
  Descript: "No."
  
  Gold:     "(Uh [FP]) oh, hi!"
  Descript: "No."

Why it happens:
  • Descript may be "cleaning up" transcription
  • Multiple speech attempts → single word selected
  • ASR choosing "final" utterance vs all attempts
  • Human gold standard captures ALL attempts + hesitations

Affected: All files, especially VR_Lady_Bird (8), VR_Falcon (7), 
          VR_Dodgeball (7), VR_Get_Out (6)

Solution: UNFIXABLE - fundamental difference in transcription philosophy:
  - Gold: Capture all disfluencies (clinical analysis)
  - Descript: Clean transcription (communication clarity)
